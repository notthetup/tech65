I usually don't do unboxing videos on Tech65.org unless it's a brand new product or it's a highly anticipated product, but I thought I'd do one for the LG Prada Phone 2 because the packaging is really well done!

In case you weren't aware, I only got to play with the phone myself (as in put it to the tests) AFTER the filming of Episode 10 of Gear65. So here are my thoughts about the device now that I've spent more time with it myself. In summary: The watch and the Prada brand are the only reasons why you'll want to get this phone. Even if you had all the money in the world.
