Prolink announced that the launch of their new gaming series of gaming peripherals will by mid-April. They will start their gaming peripherals with higher end mouse models, Hesperus and Xanthinus. The names are inspired from the bat, as the mammals is typified for its agility, resilience to forest fires and total darkness navigation.

With the announcement, there is a special customization service offered to customers in Singapore. After paying for member fees, they can opt to change the mouse top casing colours or engrave their names. Warranty status will not be affected for this change as long as they are done by Prolink. This option is for a promotional period until July 2013.

Here are the prices for Hesperus and Xanthinus

PMG9801L Hesperus High Performance Laser Gaming Mouse with weights Adjust System S$109.00
PMG9802L Xanthinus High Performance Laser Gaming Mouse S$89.00

Will be available in mid Aril 2013 in local IT Stores in Singapore

Here are the specifications

Prolink Xanthinus (PMG9802L)

Specifications
Gaming Grade Laser: 8200dpi
Ultrafast Polling: 1000hz/ms
Framerate: 12000fps
Tracking Speed: 150fps/30G
Min. Lift Off Distance: 1.5mm
Default Weight: 90g

Prolink Hesperus (PMG9801L)

Specifications
Gaming Grade Laser: 8200dpi
Ultrafast Polling: 1000hz/ms
Framerate: 12000fps
Tracking Speed: 150fps/30G
Min. Lift Off Distance: 1.5mm
Default Weight: 100g
Weight System: +36g (5.8g for each of the 6 weights)
