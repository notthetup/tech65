We take a first looks on Asus Eee Slate B121, a Windows 7 powered tablet. With the TPM chip on-board, there is an added emphasis on security, a first for a tablet. This could be a purchasing factor for companies, not so much for the consumer range. The ASUS Eee Slate B121 is available at S$1,699 (w/GST).
