We take a first look at Sony Ericsson's Xperia Neo, an Android Gingerbread device that's very similar to the Xperia Arc. The differences, thankfully, are not performance related, but with respects to physical size (the Neo is larger) and display (the Neo is slightly smaller at 3.7" and doesn't have the gapless display). That means you get similar performance at a lower cost.
