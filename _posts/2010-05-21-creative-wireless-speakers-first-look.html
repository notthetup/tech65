Creative announced a bunch of bluetooth speakers a few days ago and we were there to see them. This was Creative's attempt to build platform independent speakers, after noticing that most companies have been focusing on purely iPod/iPhone dockable speakers. These speakers also support the apt-X codec that helps reduce loss of music quality and lag when connecting via bluetooth.
