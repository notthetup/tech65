Daniel and Justin were at the HTC Hero launch in Singapore and give a sneak preview on what to expect from HTC's latest Android phone. So far I'm quite impressed by the phone. As you can see by the video, HTC created some really useful fully featured widgets like SMS, email and calendar clients that let you read your SMSes emails and events from the home screen like you would on a HTC Touch Windows Mobile phone. It's like a "build-your-own" HTC TouchFlo.

The phone will be available in brown and white from mid September on all three carriers in Singapore for a suggested retail price of S$898.
