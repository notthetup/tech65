Nokia 701 with super bright 3.5” ClearBlack display on the outside. On the inside, there’s a powerful 1 GHz processor and 8 GB of internal memory. It is running on Symbian Belle, a fresh new look with up to six home screens and NFC built in.

It is already available in Singapore, retailing at S$515.00.
