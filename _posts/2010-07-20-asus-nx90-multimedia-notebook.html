The new 18.4” ASUS NX90 multimedia notebook was launched today. The NX90 multimedia notebook unique form is designed by Mr. David Lewis, an award winning designer, and technology co-development with Bang & Olufsen ICEpower to provide full surround systems through ASUS SonicMaster sound standard.

[caption id="" align="aligncenter" width="576" caption="Polished Aluminium Body"][/caption]

The polished aluminium body reflects room ambience, adding to the atmosphere of any environment.

[caption id="" align="aligncenter" width="576" caption="A Duet of Style and Sonority - ASUS NX90"][/caption]

By placing speakers outside the chassis and alongside the screen, designers have ensured sound wraps around users and their surroundings. Meanwhile, the interface itself has been refreshed, with dual touchpads that enable true two-handed usability, creating a virtual mouse through the addition of a second touch surface. The oversized palm rests provide ample room for comfort even during prolonged sessions, one of the perks of a true multimedia desktop replacement portable PC.
