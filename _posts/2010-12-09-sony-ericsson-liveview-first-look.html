We were at the Sony Ericsson Christmas Party, spied on one of the Sony Ericsson guys holding a Sony Ericsson LiveView, and grabbed him to do a first look video for you! The “Sony Ericsson Fitness Experience Pack” is aimed at sports and fitness enthusiasts. It contains the Sony Ericsson LiveView, a sport application for the Sony Ericsson LiveView - The SportyPa Pro – and a carrying case for your mobile phone – ideal for exercising. The “Sony Ericsson Fitness Experience Pack” is currently available at all Sony Ericsson retail stores on 11th December 2010 at a recommended retail price of S$128.
