Ever wanted what Singapore's best young talents are up to? Hacking future toys for kids? Flying virtual planes with cards? Creating shapeshifters for XBox Live? Come down to the Tertiary Tech Conference and find out what keeps these talents up at night!

What is TTC?
The Tertiary Tech Conference (TTC) is an inaugural conference that aims to be the the gathering of the best projects and brightest talents from local tertiary institutions. Focusing on Games, Augmented Reality and Mobile Applications, it will feature project presentations and an exciting line-up of speakers who have been there and done that. What more, there will be demonstration booths where you can try these projects hands-on yourself and get personal with the people behind them.

Expect nothing but the best at the conference. We welcome all tertiary students, industry professionals, investors, and tech geeks to join us for a day of fun and sharing!
