We take a first looks at at the Toshiba Satellite P850. It has an Intel Core i7 processor, nVidia GT640 for the 3D and NVidia GT630 for the 2D version, both graphics processor comes with 2GB VRAM.

Th3 3D version comes in full 1920 by 1080 resolution while the 2D version comes in 1366 by 768.
