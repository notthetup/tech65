LG 3.0, PRADA and LG's latest collaboration, the third in a series of fashion smartphones, will be available for pre-order here in January 2012. The pre-order comes with a PRADA-branded accessory with the phone. Pre-Ordering will be available at all three local carriers websites from 7 January 2012. The PRADA Phone by LG 3.0 will be available at S$1,088 without contract.

The classic black smartphone features a glossy touchscreen and PRADA's signature Saffiano pattern on the back.

Specifications

  • Exclusive grey and black themed interface created specially in collaboration with PRADA
  • NOVA high brightness display -- big and bright screen at 4.3 inch and capable of displaying at 800-nit
  • 8.5mm thin using LG's signature Floating Mass Technology
  • 1.0 GHz Dual-Core/Dual-Channel architecture for high speed performance
  • Dual-band WIFI for high-speed mobile surfing
  • 8MP camera with 1080p video recording
  • Android 2.3 Gingerbread with 4.0 Ice Cream Sandwich available via update

There will be a one-day-only sale on 21 January 2012 exclusively at LG LIVE Store located at Marina Bay Sands. You would also be able to get the limited edition PRADA cradle while stocks last at the LG LIVE Store during this one-day-only sale.
