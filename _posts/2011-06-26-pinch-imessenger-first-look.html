At the ad:tech Singapore 2011 Event we got a demo of the Pinch iMessenger from Affle. A cross platform, social (and location based) messaging app for iOS, Symbian, Blackberry, Windows Mobile, Android and Web.

Pinch is $0.99 for iOS, and free on Android. It was still in private beta for Windows Mobile and Symbian at the time of this video.
