Somewhere between Joo Koon and Joo Chiat, the Tech65 crew gather in a cosy Starbucks cafe and discuss the Tech news for the week. They talk about the faster broadband from Starhub which head office in Singapore is somewhere between Joo Koon and Joo Chiat. They also discuss about the new iPhone price war in Singapore between Singtel, M1 and Starhub which is happening mainly in stores between Joo Koon and Joo Chiat. Then they go about and discuss about the widely debated Joo Joo tablet, which we suspect is name after Joo Koon and Joo Chiat.
