We had the opportunity to talk to Mr. Tony Park, Vice President of Red 5 Singapore. Red 5 is the creator of FireFall, a free to play team-based sci-fi action shooter MMO game, will be releasing in December 2011 for the public to play. This is one of the long waited game and had received many positive response from beta tester.
