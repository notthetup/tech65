Samsung Galaxy Beam will be retailing in Singapore this Saturday, July 7th 2012, at the price of S$648 (inclusive of 7% GST/without contract).

Here's a quick run through on the specifications.
1Ghz Dual-Core Processor
768MB RAM + 8GB Internal Memory, MicroSD slot (up to 32GB)
Android 2.3 Gingerbread (as Winston Goh mentioned on the video, it is upgradeable to Android 4.0 Ice Cream Sandwich)
4.0" 480x800 (WVGA) TFT
2000mAh Battery
