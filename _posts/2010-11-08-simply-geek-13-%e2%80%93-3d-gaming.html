On this Episode of Simply Geek, we have a special interview with Carl Callewaert from Fund3D games. Tech65's Justin met Carl Callewaert (Game Instructor/Director of Animation/Owner Fundi3D) and had a great chat with him about 3D Gaming industry, developing 3D Games and the technology behind 3D (not the geeky glasses type, but the stuff that's used to make things like WoW such games). They talk about the current status of 3D Gaming and where things might go in the future.
