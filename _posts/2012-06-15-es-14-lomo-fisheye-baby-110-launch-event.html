The Lomo Fisheye Baby 110 Basic and Metal versions were unveiled at the Lomography Gallery Store.

Lomo Fisheye Baby 110 Basic - S$68
Lomo Fisheye Baby 110 Metal - S$95

Technical Specs
Film type: 110 format film
Exposure area: 17 x 13mm
Lenses: full fisheye plastic lens (13mm diameter)
Focusing: focus-free
Aperture: fixed f/8
Shutter: N (1/100) and B (bulb - manual exposure)
Viewfinder: built-in
Tripod mount: no
Flash connection: Nil (Basic version)/PC Socket (Metal version)
