This just in! The red iPod nano has FINALLY arrived in Singapore. I think it's really pretty, don't you think? It'll make a perfect christmas gift *hint hint*. This special edition iPod is part of the "Product red" project. This means that for every red nano you buy, they'll donate a certain amount to help find a cure for AIDS. It's S$348 for the 4GB model and S$428 for the 8GB model.
