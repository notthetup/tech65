On this episode we take a look at Samsung continuing to blur the line between phone and tablet with the Galaxy Tab 7.0 Plus, an Android Honeycomb 3.2 device. When Samsung released their first Android tablet, the original 7 inch Galaxy Tab, it could already make phone calls, assuming you used it as a speakerphone or with a headset. But on this model, Samsung took it further by including a phone speaker on the top of the display just like any other phone. Quite amusing, if you ask me.
