TriQuint Semiconductor, Inc (NASDAQ: TQNT), officially opened TriQuint International Pte Ltd as its new International Headquarters in Singapore. The facility will be the focal point for most international customers, suppliers and manufacturing partners.

TriQuint International Pte Ltd is located at Changi Business Park, close to Singapore's Changi International Airport. The office will offer dedicated resources in a wide variety of areas ranging from customer service to supply chain management.

TriQuint Semiconductor has just completed its sixth consecutive year of record revenue, largely driven by the proliferation of smartphones, tablets and the emergence of 3G/4G broadband wireless.

We speak with Glen Riley, VP & MD of TriQuint International Pte Ltd to find out more about the company and its range of products.
