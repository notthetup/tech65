We were at The PC Show 2010 today.

[caption id="" align="aligncenter" width="576" caption="Starting Crowd of The PC Show 2010"][/caption]

Look at the crowd; they were here much earlier before the opening of The PC Show 2010. If you need some gadget or computer stuff, donâ€™t wait for the last day, the item might run out judging from the number of people queuing before the opening.

There are more than 600 exhibitors this year where leading exhibitors include Acer, Apple, ASUS, Cisco, Canon, Compaq, Dell, HP, Fujitsu, Lenovo, LG, Samsung, Sony, Toshiba and many others.

The occupying halls are on Level 1, 3, 4 and 6. (Floor Plan)

[caption id="" align="alignleft" width="259" caption="iPad Accessories"][/caption]

For Apple fans, there is EpiCenter at level 3 and many others iPhone and iPad accessories at level 6. Even through iPad is not officially launched in Singapore, but the accessories are still easily available.

Level 4 is mainly occupy by the big players in laptop and camera like Sony, HP, Acer, Samsung, Fujitsu, Dell, Canon, Casio, LG, Compaq, Lenovo, Epson, etc

For Photographers, they might want to check out Sony New Alpha NEX 3 and 5 which launched this month. Of course there are also many great bargains for digital cameras. There are Canon, Olympus, Sony, Fujitsu, etc where they will be more than willing to explain to you on the difference when choosing your camera.

[caption id="" align="aligncenter" width="259" caption="Sony Alpha Nex"][/caption]

Do check out the freebies that go with the purchase of a notebook from the vendors.

Level 6 showcase more on computer electronics products like notebook, computer accessories, etc.

Singapore 3 main Telco as occupy the largest area at level 6. There are many mobile deals going around and you might just find the right plan and phone.

[caption id="" align="aligncenter" width="576" caption="M1 Booth @ The PC Show 2010"][/caption]

[caption id="" align="aligncenter" width="576" caption="SingTel Booth @ The PC Show 2010"][/caption]

Samsung Galaxy S, the phone that many people have been talking about can also be purchase at SingTel.

[caption id="" align="aligncenter" width="259" caption="Samsung Galaxy S"][/caption]

Also Creative is showing casing their new Zii wireless speakers. Do check out the difference in the sound quality and the difference with A2DP dongle and Bluetooth connection.

[caption id="" align="aligncenter" width="259" caption="Creative @ The PC Show 2010"][/caption]

SingTel improvised on their Re-contract Telephone Booth which keeps people wondering what it is supposed to do. There is no need to get into the main queue for re-contracting, this definitely shorten the waiting time.

Last but not least, you might want to print out the floor plan before heading to Suntec. The PC Show 2010 will only be available up to Sunday 13 June 2010, and the opening hours are from 12pm to 9pm daily.
