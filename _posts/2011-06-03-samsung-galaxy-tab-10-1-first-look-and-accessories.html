We've already did a first look of the Samsung Galaxy Tab 10.1v back in March during the Samsung Forum 2011, but this is the updated retail version of the Samsung Galaxy Tab 10.1 that will be available in Singapore. All the specifications are the same except for the sleek body and weight of 560g. We also go through briefly all the various accessories available for the Samsung Galaxy Tab 10.1.

The Samsung Galaxy Tab 10.1 will be available in Singapore some time in July with the complete Samsung TouchWiz UI at the following retail price:

Samsung Galaxy Tab 10.1 (3G)
16GB - S$848
32GB - S$978
64GB - S$1108

Samsung Galaxy Tab 10.1 (WiFi)
16GB - S$668
32GB - S$798
64GB - S$928
