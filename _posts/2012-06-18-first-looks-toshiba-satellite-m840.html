We take a first looks on the Toshiba Satellite M840. The M840 has a 14" screen and weighs 1.99 kilograms.

It also comes with different configurations of the Intel Core i3, i5 and i7 processors and features the AMD 7670M graphics card with 2 gigabytes of video RAM.
