Justin got a chance to check out the new Sony Ericsson txt recently at the Sony Ericsson press event. It's a messaging phone, and it features a full view QWERTY keyboard and SMS shortcut key along with Facebook and Twitter integration. The txt sports a 2.6" 240px 320p screen and has a 3.2mp camera.

The Sony Ericsson txt will be available globally in selected markets from Q3 2011. More on the Sony Ericsson press website.
