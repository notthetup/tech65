This is a pretty neat accessory that all notebook users would be able to appreciate. Hooking up just a single USB 2.0 cable to your notebook when you get home or get to your office and getting everything from video out to ethernet immediately. What's so cool is that because it's a USB 2.0 product, you can use it on any Windows machine of any brand. And since DisplayLink drivers (which the device is based on) are available for Macs, you can probably get it to work on a Mac too! (Emphasis on the "probably")
