We got a chance to take a close look (a bit too close) at the INQ Mini 3G, a brand new feature phone designed specifically for casual users that still want to be connected to their social network at all times. The INQ Mini 3G, unlike Sony Ericsson's C510, actually integrates Facebook into the phone's messaging, address book and camera functionality rather than just provide an app or a link to the Facebook web application. It also has a built-in twitter client as well as integration with Singtel's new AMPed music store and a Windows Live IM client.

This is probably the first time you'll get to take a look at INQ's brand new phone launched with Singtel in full HD video, as Singapore's the first country in the world to get this phone! Brought to you, of course, by the friendly Tech65.org crew...
