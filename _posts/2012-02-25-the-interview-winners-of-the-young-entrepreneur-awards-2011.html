The grand winner for this year's YEA is Mr Beh Weng Wei. First runner-up is Mr Bryan Lee. Second runner-up winners are Mr Zhang Weiliang and Mr Joshua Chao. A Special Mention was also awarded to to Mr Keith Tan.

Speaking more about the Young Entrepreneurs Award, Mr Andrew Koh, Senior Director and General Manager of Consumer Imaging and Information Products Group, Canon Singapore said,"The young Entrepreneurs Awards is just one of the programmes in our THINK BIG THINK CANON campaign, which we launched in 2010 to help empower entrepreneurs to attain a world-class level of business excellence. We were impressed by the quality of submissions this year, and look forward to seeing the winner's business proposals being turned into successful ventures."

The Young Entrepreneurs Award is jointly organised by Canon and MediaCorp.
