At the HP Fall 2009 launch event in Singapore, HP announced a special edition of the HP Mini 110. This netbook features a 3D imprint cover designed by Tord Boontje, and has newer software such as QuickWeb (instant web access without booting up) and Quicksync. This netbook will also feature Windows 7 starter edition.
