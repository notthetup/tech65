We had the opportunity to play with the new LG Optimus 2X, one of the world's first dual-core mobile phones in the market running Android 2.2 (Froyo). The LG Optimus 2X will be retailing in Singapore at all telcos starting from end of March at a suggested retail price of SGD$888 without contract.
