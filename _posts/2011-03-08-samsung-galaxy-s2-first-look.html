Justin and I head down to the Samsung Forum 2011, and got a chance to get our hands on the new Samsung Galaxy S II, the predecessor successor to Samsung's successful Android phone. The new Galaxy S II features a slightly larger 4.27" Super AMOLED Plus display (I expect to see the Super AMOLED Plus Ultra next year), a dual-core processor and a 8-megapixel camera.
