Nokia N9 First Look Updates

Price & Availability

The Nokia N9 will be retailing in all 3 operators, SingTel, M1 and Starhub at:

  • 16GB at S$799, available in three colors black, cyan and magenta
  • 64GB at S$899, available in only black colour
