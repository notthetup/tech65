A few weeks ago, at a blogger event, the Nokia Singapore team showcased the Nokia N9 to the bloggers present.

We took this opportunity to find out more about several features of the Nokia N9.

The Nokia N9 will be available at Nokia retail stores from 15 October 2011.

The Nokia N9 is available in three colors – black, cyan and magenta, with storage options of 16GB and 64GB to accommodate plenty of content. The Nokia N9 16GB will be retailing in all three colours at SGD799 (RRP) and 64GB in black at SGD899 (RRP). All 3 operators in Singapore – Singtel, Starhub and M1 – will offer the Nokia N9 with attractive data plans.

Nokia will provide support for the Nokia N9 until at least 2015, including software updates, care and services support.
