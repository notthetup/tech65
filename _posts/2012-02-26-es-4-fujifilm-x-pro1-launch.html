Launch of Fujifilm X-Pro1.

The X-Pro1 features a 16 megapixel APS-C X-Trans CMOS sensorTM, a brand new X lens mount, Hybrid Multi Viewfinder and three prime lenses.

 

Pricing and availability

The recommended retail pricing for X-Pro1 is as following:

  • Body: S$2400 (inclusive of 7% GST)
  • Basic lens: S$900 to S$1000 (inclusive of 7% GST)
  • X-Pro1 will be available in all major electronics retailers in Singapore in March 2012.
