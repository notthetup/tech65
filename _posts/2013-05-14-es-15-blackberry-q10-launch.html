Launch of the BlackBerry Q10.

Useful Facts:

  • Display 3.1”, Super AMOLED display, 720x720 resolution at 330 PPI
  • Dual Core 1.5 GHz Processor
  • Camera: 8 MP rear facing camera, 3x digital zoom 720p HD video recording 2MP front facing camera, 3x digital zoom 720p HD video recording
  • Ports: microHDMI out port and microUSB charging port
  • WiFi: Dual Band 802.11 a/b/g/n, Mobile Hotspot
  • Network Connectivity: 4G LTE or HSPA+, support for global roaming

Battery Life

  • Talk Time: Up to 13.5 hours on 3G
  • Standby Time: Up to 354 hours on 3G, up to 349 hours on 2G
  • Audio Playback: Up to 61 hours
  • Video Playback: Up to 9 hours

It will be retailing in Singapore by end of May 2013 at the recommended retail price of SGD$898.
