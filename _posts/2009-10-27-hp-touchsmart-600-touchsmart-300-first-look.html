Back at the HP Fall 2009 launch event in Singapore, HP announced the HP Touchsmart 600 and Touchsmart 300, an update to their line of all-in-one touchscreen computers. With these two computers, they introduced a brand new version of their Touchsmart software that finally allows developers to build custom tiles for people to download and install. This new Touchsmart software is also a lot more customisable than before, and has more powerful tiles that are more than just shortcuts to external applications.
