We finally get our hands on the Sony Vaio P, and present it to you in an unbox video. Will it exceed the initial expectations it gave us when we first saw it? Why don't we start with the unboxing first, shall we?

So far, the device has proven that even though it's running on Windows Vista, it is usable and doesn't lag. As long as Aero is off (and unlike Windows 7, Aero doesn't do anything extra other than look cool), the device can run quite smoothly. In fact, some of the advantages of Windows Vista is proving to help the Vaio, especially with regards to connecting to the internet wirelessly by tethering to your bluetooth enabled 3G phone. I'm actually liking it so far. Will that change? Well, I guess you'll just have to wait for the next episode of Gear65 to find out!
