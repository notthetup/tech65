Highlights of GALAXY Nexus (to be available in February 2012 at S$948)
· World's first smartphone running on Android™4.0, Ice Cream Sandwich, the latest release of the Android platform
· Features a 4.65" HD Super AMOLED display at 720p resolution and an ultra-fast 1.2GHz dual core processor to take advantage of GALAXY Nexus' enhanced multitasking capabilities
· Redesigned user interface with improved multi-tasking, notifications, Wi-Fi hotspot, NFC support and a full web browsing experience
· Designed to provide a "pure Google" experience, GALAXY Nexus will be the first to receive software upgrades and new applications as soon as they are available
