Huawei showcase Huawei MediaPad yesterday at Huawei Media Preview. Huawei MediaPad is the first Android tablet that announce to feature the latest Android Honeycomb 3.2, powered by Qualcomm's dual-core 1.2GHz processor with a 7" IPS LCD capacitive touchscreen.

Huawei MediaPad will be launching in 3rd quarter 2011 and the pricing is yet to finalize.
